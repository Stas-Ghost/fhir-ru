Quality Improvement Core (QICore) Implementation Guide

This implementation guide is prepared as a U.S. Realm Specification on behalf of the Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services and the U.S. Office of the National Coordinator (ONC). Much of the content is likely to be usable in other jurisdictions.

Draft Standard for Trial Use (DSTU)

This FHIR Implementation Guide is undergoing a DSTU ballot. The focus of the ballot is limited in scope to the IG, the profiles, operations, value sets and conformance statements it defines, although comments on the underlying resources will also be accepted. This guide will be balloted separately from the FHIR specification itself.

Specification

The QICore FHIR Implementation Guide is built on top of the HL7 FHIR standard. The QICore FHIR IG implements the Quality Information and Clinical Knowledge (QUICK) logical model, balloted separately. Each QICore profile implements a class in the QUICK model. The background of the QUICK model and its relationship to existing quality data standards, such as the Quality Data Model (QDM), is explained elsewhere. The table below provides a top-level mapping QUICK classes to QICore profiles, along with the identification of the base FHIR resource.
