Руководства по реализации, входящие в спецификацию FHIR

Руководство по реализации представляет собой коллекцию профилей, требований соответствия, наборов значений (ValueSets) и других ресурсов вместе с текстовыми указаниями, содержащими инструкции по использованию FHIR в определенной области задач. Руководства по реализации могут разрабатываться и публиковаться в составе версии спецификации FHIR или в виде отдельной спецификации. Дополнительные инструменты и средства поддержки руководств по реализации продолжают разрабатываться, поэтому предлагаемые материалы следует рассматривать как "предварительные" с точки зрения публикации.

At present, implementation guides are published in conjunction with the FHIR specification because tooling that supports straightforward independent publication is not yet developed. However, the intention is that in the future implementation guides will be published separately from the core FHIR specification and can be subject to separate approval processes.

The predominance of US implementation guides in this specification is a result of the fact that the US doesn't have an independent affiliate body to support balloting and publishing of US realm-specific content. It should in no way be interpreted as suggesting that FHIR is a US-centric specification.

Название Использование
Argonaut Project A joint task force of the HIT Standards and Policy Committees: a first-generation FHIR-based API and Core Data Services specification
Data Access Framework A U.S. Realm guide for making use of FHIR resources to support queries between systems within an enterprise and across enterprises.
EHRS Functional model - Record Lifecycle Events Describes the use of the Provenance and AuditEvent resources to support meeting the needs of the EHRS functional model requirements for tracking record lifecycle.
Quality Improvement Core Profiles An implementation guide for making use of FHIR resources in clinical quality measures and clinical decision support applications. Developed for the U.S. Realm, but more broadly applicable.
Структурированный сбор данных Американское руководство по использованию Data Elements, Questionnaires и QuestionnaireAnswers для поддержки предзаполнения и автозаполнения форм.
Structured Data Capture - Data Element Exchange A U.S. Realm guide for supporting the exchange and maintenance of Data Elements by and between data element registries.
US Laboratory Order A US Realm guide making use of Diagnostic Order and FHIR resources referenced by them to support ordering of laboratory tests in ambulatory care.
US Laboratory Report A US Realm guide making use of Diagnostic Report, and FHIR resources referenced by them to support reporting of laboratory tests in ambulatory care.
US Laboratory Report to Public Health A US Realm guide making use of Diagnostic Report, and FHIR resources referenced by them to support reporting of reportable lab tests to Public Health jurisdictions.
